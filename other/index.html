About

Welcome to Zazi's Guide to financial literacy and economics. This site is meant to empower individuals with the knowledge and tools they need to make informed financial decisions and achieve economic well-being. This site is designed to make resources accessible for everyone.

Badge Key

Adults - Intended for adult learners

Teens - Intended for teenage learners

Kids - Intended for young learners

M - Available in multiple languages

- US-specific economics or content

- International economics or content

Website - Website resource

Online Course - Online course resource

Video - Video or webinar resource

Blog - Blog or article resource

PDF - PDF or downloadable resource

E-Book - E-book or digital reading material

15m - Resource duration: 15 minutes

20m - Resource duration: 20 minutes

30m - Resource duration: 30 minutes

40m - Resource duration: 40 minutes

V - Visual Learners (requires images, graphics, or videos)

A - Auditory Learners (requires listening or spoken content)

K - Kinesthetic Learners (hands-on, interactive, or physical activities)
